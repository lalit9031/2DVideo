# FRUIT CARTOON CHANNEL — CHARACTER ACTING GUIDE
### (Companion to the Character Bible. Use this to keep each character's MOVEMENT, GESTURES, and EMOTIONAL DELIVERY consistent across every video — not just their look.)

---

## WHY THIS MATTERS FOR AUTOMATION

Visual consistency (Character Bible) keeps a character *looking* the same.
This guide keeps them *behaving* the same — so Appy always gestures like Appy, Ozzy always moves like Ozzy, no matter which script or video they appear in.

When writing Phase 6 (image-to-video prompts), pull the relevant character's **signature gesture bank** and **default movement style** below instead of inventing new mannerisms each time. Only the *specific* gesture used per line should change — the *style* of how they move never should.

---

## HOW TO DESCRIBE A CHARACTER'S ACTING (3-part formula)

For every speaking moment, combine:
1. **Movement style** (how they generally hold/move their body — fixed per character)
2. **Signature gesture** (pick one from their gesture bank, matched to the emotional beat of the line)
3. **Emotional delivery** (their default range + the scene's specific emotion)

Example: *"Appy — steady grounded stance, places hand over heart, speaks with quiet sincerity"*

## CHARACTER ACTING PROFILES

### Appy (Apple) — The Loyal Hero
- **Movement style:** Grounded, steady, slightly slow — moves like someone who thinks before acting
- **Signature gestures:** hand over heart / offers open palm to others / rubs back of neck when unsure / steps forward protectively
- **Emotional default range:** Warm, sincere, quietly hurt rather than explosive
- **Voice pacing:** Slow, deliberate, gentle pauses between phrases
- **Idle behavior (when not speaking):** stands still, attentive, occasionally nods

---

### Nana (Banana) — The Bright Achiever
- **Movement style:** Quick, bouncy, energetic — moves with visible enthusiasm
- **Signature gestures:** claps hands together / bounces on heels / spins slightly when excited / points forward with confidence
- **Emotional default range:** Upbeat and expressive, but crumples quickly when hurt (big emotional swings)
- **Voice pacing:** Fast, bright, rising intonation
- **Idle behavior:** shifts weight foot to foot, can't stay fully still

### Ozzy (Orange) — The Charismatic Schemer
- **Movement style:** Smooth, controlled, minimal wasted motion — deliberately cool
- **Signature gestures:** adjusts collar/blazer / steeples fingers / slow head tilt when calculating / slight smirk-and-turn exit
- **Emotional default range:** Composed and confident on the surface, brief flashes of coldness in eyes
- **Voice pacing:** Even, unhurried, slightly drawn-out on key words (persuasive rhythm)
- **Idle behavior:** stands with arms loosely crossed, watching others

## USING THIS IN PHASE 6 VIDEO PROMPTS

Replace generic gesture placeholders with the character's locked profile. Example upgrade:

**Before (generic):**
"SPEAKER 1: AppyHero — mouth moving in lip-sync, raises hand — speaks in warm tone"

**After (acting-guide matched):**
"SPEAKER 1: AppyHero — grounded steady stance, places hand over heart — mouth moving in lip-sync — speaks in warm, sincere, slow-paced tone saying '[line]'"

This keeps every character's body language recognizable across dozens of future videos, the same way their face and outfit stay recognizable.
